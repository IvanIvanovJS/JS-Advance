Deep Breathing to Reduce Stress

Type: Breathing Technique

Difficulty: Easy

Practice deep, slow breathing to reduce tension and improve focus. Inhale through your nose for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, and exhale through your mouth for 8 seconds.
